Recycling

Recycling reduces greenhouse gas emissions by cutting down the need for energy-intensive raw material extraction and processing. Using recycled materials requires less energy, lowering carbon emissions. Additionally, recycling minimizes waste in landfills, which decreases methane production. Overall, recycling conserves resources and helps combat climate change.

Transportation

Adopting cleaner transportation methods, like electric vehicles and public transit, significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions. Electric vehicles produce zero tailpipe emissions, and public transportation reduces the number of individual car journeys. Enhancing fuel efficiency and promoting cycling or walking can further decrease emissions, contributing to a sustainable future.
